About US

Lo primero que quiero decir es que esta página web está basada en el poyecto de Empresa e Iniciativa Emprendedora del curso 2023/2024

We are a newly created computer store that sells both in person and online

We want to be one of the largest IT companies in Spain over time and if possible go further.

History

The three of us had different business ideas, such as a gym, a bank... But none of us were convinced by any of those ideas, the only one we agreed on was a computer store.

However, we also came to the conclusion that a computer store was something simple and was going to fall short.

We all came up with the idea of implementing a website to sell the products and we also came up with the idea of adding a section where unemployed technicians can register to support them and we can get remuneration from them.
